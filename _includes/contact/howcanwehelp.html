How Can We Help?

Contacting TOPS and engaging with the community through email or GitHub discussions opens the door to valuable insights and collaborative opportunities in the world of open science education.

Start a Discussion on GitHub

If you have questions or insights about Open Science or TOPS, don't hesitate to start a discussion on our GitHub. Open and constructive dialogues in this space are incredibly valuable and helps foster a community of like-minded individuals who are committed to advancing open science practices.

Start Discussion

Send Us An Email

Sending an email to the TOPS team is an excellent way to get answers to questions about taking Open Science 101, teaching Open Science 101, open science stories, or anything related to open science. Direct your emails to hq-openscience@nasa.gov or click the button below to get started in your email app.
